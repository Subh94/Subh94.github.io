Why I Moved to GraphDatabase for IOT Project

Let’s start this by some quick question

  • Why IOT is becoming more popular now days
  • Who is fulling IOT device intelligent
  • What happen to IOT products if they will become isolated to themselves

If you are able to think / answer this, you will realize IOT is fulled by connecting all your devices, application and data. It’s in fact graph of your all data, as a example let’s think about UBER, it’s not a mere mobile application to book cab/taxi, in fact it’s a graph of all user and taxi service provider, acting as a smart service collecting, storing , processing large amount of data from large number of smartphones connected to user.

these phone acting as a IOT devices, sensing location related to user cab/taxi service provider, mixing them and providing a optimized match for resources consumption.

Let’s talk about another Company “Google”, Google photos is using graph data to providing analysis of stored photos from unrelated data. It doesn’t sell storage for photos, but it sell advertisement and analysis outcome of photos. Let’s say they find how to make profile pic more better or some more way to make photos quality better, then they can get income out of that.

What role graph database can play in IOT scenario

Till now you got that IOT can only become more intelligent and successful when all data will be connected to each other and we can find relation between them, then we can make those service more useful for people and smart.

It’s not a matter that Relational Database don’t have relation, in fact they have, but they are fix for preplanned process and outcome.

IOT service value through unseen matches or relation between data for great business outcome, achieving using half of work on how we are storing data, and half how we are processing them.
